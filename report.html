Case Study: A Toronto Casual Dining Spot

The Challenge: A popular Toronto restaurant was seeing labor costs consistently hover above the industry average of 30%, costing them an estimated $2,000 per month. The owner was spending over 10 hours a month on manual scheduling.

The Margin Mind Solution: We analyzed their historical sales and staffing data, integrating it with local event calendars and weather forecasts. Our predictive model optimized their schedules to match real-time demand.

The Result

Within 4 months, labor costs were reduced to a stable 31%, resulting in an estimated $22,000 in annual savings. The owner also saved 10-12 hours per month on administrative tasks, reinvesting that time into improving customer experience.
